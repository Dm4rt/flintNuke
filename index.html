Welcome to Bedrock Island Facility

Flintstones Nuclear Research (FNR) operates a remote experimental reactor and radar test range on Bedrock Island. Our mission is to explore the peaceful use of advanced pebble-bed reactors and long-range detection systems.

Facility Coordinates: 26° 32' 19" N, 54° 00' 42" E

Research Focus

Our teams investigate radiation-hardened electronics, coastal early-warning radars, and small-modular reactors for prehistoric power grids.
